About Me

Hailing from the quaint town of Deoghar in Jharkhand, India, I find inspiration in the simplest of things. Currently, I'm a dedicated 10th-grade student at St. Xavier High School, where I'm nurturing my passions and aspirations.

A Passion for Programming

One of my greatest passions is programming. I find the world of coding to be a canvas of endless possibilities. It's here that I can bring my ideas to life, creating digital solutions that make an impact. From crafting lines of code to developing functional software, programming is both my playground and my sanctuary.

Dreams Beyond the Horizon

As I stand on the threshold of my future, I have a soaring dream to become a commercial pilot. The sky beckons with its boundless beauty and challenges, and I'm determined to conquer the skies. With unwavering dedication, I'm working towards turning this dream into my reality, one step at a time.

Balancing Heights and Books

Juggling between school books and flight manuals, my journey is a blend of academic rigor and a yearning for the skies. St. Xavier High School provides me with not only knowledge but also the foundation to pursue my dreams. Each lesson I learn takes me a step closer to the cockpit.

Beyond the Classroom
