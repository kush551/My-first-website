ABOUT ME

MY STORY

My journey into the world of programming has been nothing short of fascinating. For me, it's not merely about typing lines of code; it's about using the enchantment of programming languages to transform my thoughts into reality.

My coding journey took flight during the lockdown of 2020. While the world faced unprecedented challenges, I decided to invest my time in learning something new.

That's when I enrolled in basic coding classes. Little did I know that this decision would spark a deep passion for coding within me.

I love Code

Coding isn't just a skill I've acquired; it's a creative outlet that allows me to breathe life into my ideas. While others may brainstorm leave their concepts hanging, I'm the kind of person who revels in the art of coding. Every line I write is a step toward transforming abstract ideas into tangible solutions.

Coding is my canvas of creativity. It's the medium through which I express myself. Whether I'm crafting a visually stunning website, developing a functional app, or tackling complex problems, each keystroke brings me closer to realizing my visions.
